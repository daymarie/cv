Develop your Skill Sets

"When you feel that - what you're really happy about is a particular thing that you do."

Then maybe you can boost that and make something out of it! Develop your Skill Sets!

Sometimes, you get so influenced by what looks good based with other people's judgement, what others have to say about you before you do something for a living, a work, or a job to apply with. I would advise that you do it for your couple of reasons as to Why are you doing it in the first place? Why do you want to do it? Do you really want to do it? Think about it, it's not for them, it's for you.

So what? if the work you want isn't something that is looking good for others? Will their judgement be something that will bring your needs or food into your table? Will listening to them be a benefit to you than losing self belief? If you look into all that this way, at the end of the day you can determine if you WILL really stick to the type of job or work you want to do.

Like for example you saw a kid vending fish around, assuming you ask yourself - why is the kid selling when they're supposed to be taken care of at home? Is that kid not shy of selling fish in the neighborhood too, just to earn money for food? Think about it!

Even kids are shy about what other people have to say about them. If crushes pass by, what will they say about them?(you know kids now a days) How do people look in their eyes? What are they called of what they're doing? A lot more you can think about... but why would a kid do that?.... That's more probably an example of how others may see you, question you and judge you for what you're trying to work with.
